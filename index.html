Game Development is the process of creating games and describes the design, development and release of a game. It involves concept generation, design, build, test and release. When creating a game , a team thinks about rewards in the game, player engagement and level design. There are many roles in game development and you can be a programmer, a sound designer, an artist, or many other roles. Game development can be done by a team or by a single individual. Many developers choose to develop a game using a game development engine.Game engines can make the process of creating a game much easier and enable developers to reuse lots of functionality. It also takes care of rendering for 2D and 3D graphics, physics and collision detection, sound, and scripting.
